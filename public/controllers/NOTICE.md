# Controller artwork attribution

The controller diagram SVGs in this folder (`xbox.svg`, `playstation.svg`,
`nintendo.svg`) are the "VSCView" full-controller overlays (Xbox Wireless,
DualShock 4, Switch Pro) from the **Gamepad Asset Pack** by AL2009man, used
under the MIT License.

- Source: https://github.com/AL2009man/Gamepad-Asset-Pack
- License: MIT

The `*.wire.svg` files are generated from those sources by
`scripts/wireify-controllers.mjs` (fills stripped, shapes stroked) to render the
emulator controller-layout diagram as a wireframe while keeping every button,
symbol and logo.
